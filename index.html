Frequently Asked Questions

Is my audio data secure?

Yes. Both tools are built on WebAudio and run locally in your browser. Your audio stays on your device unless you choose to export or share.

Which browsers work best?

Latest Chrome/Edge/Brave/Opera offer the best AudioWorklet performance. Recent Firefox is supported; Safari compatibility varies.

Can I use this offline?

Yes. Add to Home Screen on mobile/desktop to install as a PWA. With a service worker, both can work offline after first load.

Any tips for better audio quality?

Prefer wired microphones/interfaces, close other tabs, and set sample rate to 44.1/48kHz. On Windows, WASAPI Exclusive or ASIO via a bridge can help.
